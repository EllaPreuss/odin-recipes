Homemade Bread

Description

Homemade bread is the best kind of bread because you control the process and what goes into it. Give this bread a try! I promise it's easy.

Stop buying bread and make your own, the results will surprise you!

Ingredients:

  • 500 grs of all purpose flour
  • 1 packet of instant yeast
  • 350 ml of warm water
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil (or any light oil)
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, dissolve the sugar in warm water and then stir in yeast. Allow to proof until yeast resembles a creamy foam, about 5 minutes.
  2. Mix salt and oil into the yeast. Mix in flour one cup at a time.
  3. Knead dough for 7 minutes in the bowl of your standing mixer with the dough attachment. Then, place in a well oiled bowl, and turn dough to coat in oil. Cover with a damp cloth. Allow to rise until doubled in bulk, about 30 minutes to 1 hour, depending on your room temperature.
  4. Punch dough down. Knead for 1 minute and divide in half. Shape into loaves and place into two greased 9Ã—5 inch loaf pans. Allow to rise for 30 minutes, or until dough has risen 1 inch above pans.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30-40 minutes.
  6. Cool, brush with butter and enjoy!

Homemade Strawberry Jam

Description

This summer, try out this simple jam recipe! Only 3 ingredients required.

Make jam to last you for months with a simple canning technique. This is my go-to recipe for making delicious jam. Makes 3 jars, or 2 and a half, depending on your cooking time.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg of fresh strawberries, cleaned and sliced.
  • 500 grs of sugar.
  • Juice of a lemon.

Steps:

  1. Clean your strawberries and cut them in small slices. Put them in a medium to big pot.
  2. Add the sugar and lemon juice. Mix together.
  3. Turn on the stove, on high heat until it starts boiling. Reduce heat to low once it boils.
  4. Cook on low heat for 30 minutes, mixing occasionally so it doesn't stick to the pot.
  5. Check the consistency on a plate. If it's too runny, cook for 10 more minutes. If it reached the desired consistency, turn the stove off.
  6. Let it cool slightly before putting it in the jars. If you didn't do the canning process, the jam will only last for a month on the shelf.
